Daily Burn & Daily Burn 365

Daily Burn 365 is an interactive, high intensity live workout class airing daily, on demand, year round from any device. The class encourages people to work out, stay accountable and create a fun and vibrant community. The visual branding takes on vibrant colors, playful photography and fun illustrations to create a balance between feel good and work hard.

Daily Burn identity was created by an outside creative agency. The challenge was to create and keep a flexible enough Daily Burn 365 brand system to grow and develop with the brand as we created more content and partnered with brands.

The brand also extended to support illustrations and icons, color palette coordination for studio shoots and airing, a library of retouched imagery for social graphics, and design and direction for all final applications.

Daily Burn 365 kept people engaged and accountable with daily workout previews, workout summaries, and weekly newsletters. I developed an email template system to make sure that the 365 vibrant human connections were the main focus of all design elements.
