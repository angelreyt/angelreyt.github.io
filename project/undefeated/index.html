Undefeated Kickboxing

Undefeated is a kickboxing workout series for people want a challenging high intensity workout. It was important for the branding to reflect a sense of intimacy, strength, and perseverance. The brand challenge was to encourage people to expand their fitness limits even when working out solo. The identity was translated onto a variety of media such as email campaigns, landing pages, and social graphics.

Story, Photo Direction: Zack McTee
Photography: Ryan Kelly

Variations of the logo were created, each different from each other to capture the air of unwavering relentlessness, strong character and graceful ruggedness of the program. The final logo mark was inspired by cursive lettering and the juxtaposition of street graffiti art.
