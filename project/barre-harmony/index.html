Barre Harmony

Barre Harmony is a unique barre and dance fusion program created by Daily Burn. The program and brand identity celebrate the ease and refinement of body movements by giving a minimalistic-like look to the logo mark and muted but luxurious color palette to the graphics. I led the creative direction, created the branding and collateral for this project.
