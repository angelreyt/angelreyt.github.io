Barre Harmony

Creative direction, branding and website was created for Barre Harmony, a stand alone Barre and dance fusion program created by Daily Burn. The brand identity celebrates the ease and refinement of body movements by giving a minimalistic-like look to the logo mark and muted but luxurious color palette to the graphics.

Photography: Ryan Kelly

Many variations of the icon were made but I always thought they left too much to interpret. The logotype I created was straightforward and it represented the new program in a simple and elegant way.
